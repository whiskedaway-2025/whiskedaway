About Us

Whisked Away is a home-baked adventure, created and owned by 13 year old Rainer! Established in August 2024, we started making cakes for parties, and then, in June 2025, we did our first major event in Burghead, Scotland, for a Craft Fair! Have a look at our current ranges below, or make your own cake with the custom cake builder!
